# Retail Sales AI Prompts

A beginner-friendly collection of AI prompts designed for common retail sales situations.
This repository is intended for retail staff with no technical background.

---

## Who This Is For

- Retail sales associates
- Store staff and floor executives
- Anyone handling customers in a retail environment

No coding or AI knowledge is required.

---

## How to Use These Prompts

1. Copy a prompt from this page
2. Paste it into ChatGPT or any AI assistant
3. Read the response
4. Adapt the response to your own speaking style

---

## Prompt Categories

### Price Objection

Prompt:
Act as an experienced retail sales assistant.  
A customer says, "This is too expensive."  
Give 3 polite responses that acknowledge the concern and keep the conversation friendly.  
Use simple, natural language.

---

### Just Looking

Prompt:
Act like a calm and respectful retail staff member.  
A customer says, "I'm just looking."  
Give 3 responses that keep engagement without being pushy.
